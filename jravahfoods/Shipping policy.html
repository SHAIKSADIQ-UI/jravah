Shipping Policy

Here’s how we process and deliver your handcrafted JRavah goodies.

We are pleased to offer shipping services across all locations in India.

Preparation time: Please allow 1–2 business days for the preparation of your order. We appreciate your patience and understanding, as each product is specially prepared to meet our quality standards before dispatch.

Shipping & Delivery Time: Once prepared, your order will be shipped and is expected to be delivered within 4-6 business days from the date of order placement, depending on your location and courier availability.

Free shipping cost depends upon the weight of quantities. Products ordered after 6 pm will be processed on the next business day.

Order Processing: As we operate on a made-to-order basis, we do not maintain pre-made stock. Preparation of your order will begin only after the order is successfully placed.
